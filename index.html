About TravelBug

TravelBug is a travel tracking application that helps you visualize and explore your travel history. Connect your calendar to automatically track countries, cities, and travel patterns across your journeys.

Features

  • Automatic travel detection from calendar events
  • Track countries and cities visited
  • Visualize your travel patterns
  • All data stored locally on your device
